# Roadmap

## Phase 0

- Lock the modernization and continuity positioning.
- Split browser-safe and server-safe boundaries for registry and generator.
- Restore green build and typecheck baseline.

## Phase 1

- Rehome contracts into the final nested package graph.
- Add versioned schemas for inspection, mapping, patch, registry, entitlements, governance, and Doctor outputs.

## Phase 2

- Finish token engine split and expose Quick Brand, Import Brand, pack overlays, CSS export, JSON export, and safety validators.

## Phase 3

- Split runtime into theme, a11y, motion, layout, interaction, telemetry, and performance packages.

## Phase 4

- Move primitives into final React and WC package groups.
- Keep WC as the portability floor and React as first-class DX.

## Phase 5

- Rehome page-first templates into shells, states, SaaS, marketing, docs, auth, and sample-data packages.

## Phase 6

- Finish `packages/modernization/*` and Doctor verification workflow.

## Phase 7

- Complete generator recipe split and remove remaining monolithic generator seams.

## Phase 8

- Finish tooling split for CLI, create, doctor CLI, codemod runner, and Storybook.

## Phase 9

- Migrate remaining traffic from `apps/web` and `apps/studio` to website, docs, wizard, control-plane, and sandbox.

## Phase 10

- Harden platform API persistence boundaries and rollout/governance seams without overbuilding hosted complexity.
